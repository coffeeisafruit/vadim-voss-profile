About Vadim Voss

The Story: Vadim Voss is a business professional and founder of Next Level DeFi, where he helps everyday people turn crypto from a confusing "casino" into a practical, repeatable passive-income system.

After more than a decade in the crypto markets—and over 20 years as an entrepreneur—Vadim noticed the same pattern over and over: most people don’t fail because they’re “not smart enough”… they fail because the setup is stressful, the information is scattered, and one wrong click can cost real money. So he built a simpler path.

That path became Next Level DeFi: a beginner-friendly framework that starts with clear education, then moves into a guided strategy, and—if someone wants it—hands-on help to get fully set up and running.

Today, Vadim’s mission is straightforward: make DeFi accessible, safe, and actionable for non-technical people, so they can start generating passive income without spending months on YouTube, chasing hype tokens, or guessing what to do next.

Full refund if no yield-generating position within 14 days of purchase. Vadim sets it up in the first session, so success is guaranteed as long as they book within the 14-day window. Only 1 refund request in the last JV — near-zero refund rate.
